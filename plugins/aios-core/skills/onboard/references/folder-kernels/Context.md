# Context (the brain of the system)

> [!important] Language: these instructions are in English; the user is Hebrew-speaking. Reason and run the steps in English, but write everything the user sees in Hebrew: chat replies, reports, vault notes, tables, status lines, task text. Keep commands, file paths, field names, dates, and numbers as they are.

This is the most important folder in the vault. Everything the system needs to know about you and the business in order to work like you lives here: who you are, what you sell, to whom, in what voice, and where you are headed. Every other skill reads from here before it acts. The fuller and more accurate these files are, the better everything else works. Whatever is written here for you is written in Hebrew.

## The files

Not every file exists in every vault. They are created during setup based on what you told us, and they fill in over time.

| File | What it holds |
| --- | --- |
| `me.md` | Who you are, your work style, when you are sharpest in the day, open loops |
| `business.md` | The business: structure, what it does, how it is built |
| `services.md` | What you actually sell: revenue lines, what each one delivers and to whom |
| `icp.md` | The ideal customer: role, their day, how they describe the problem in their own words |
| `pain-points.md` | Customer pains and how you solve them |
| `brand.md` | The voice, the tone, the messaging, phrases you always say and words you will not touch |
| `strategy.md` | Goals and priorities: where you are headed this year, and what you say no to |
| `infrastructure.md` | The tools, the systems, and where the truth lives for each process |

## Routing

- Your preferences, style, habits, open loops, personal details that affect the work go to `me.md`.
- Business and structure information goes to `business.md`. Services and revenue lines go to `services.md`.
- Ideal customer goes to `icp.md`, customer pains go to `pain-points.md`.
- Voice, tone, messaging, brand identity go to `brand.md`. Real writing samples of yours are gold here.
- Goals, OKRs, quarterly priorities go to `strategy.md`.
- Any change in tools, integrations, or keys goes to `infrastructure.md`.

## Rules

- These files are the **source of truth**. Do not duplicate their content elsewhere in the vault, link to them with a `[[link]]`.
- When a user shares information that changes context (a preference, a change in the business, a new goal), update the right file here immediately, without asking permission, and report what was updated.
- Do not create an empty file here. A file is born when it has real content.
- Write in the voice of a teammate: specific names, specific numbers, never generic.
- Use `[[wikilinks]]` for every person, company, project, or system mentioned.
